Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the extension need a separate script?

X.com serves videos using streamed chunks (blob URLs) rather than direct MP4 files. The extension intercepts the network requests and React properties in the page context to retrieve the official MP4 source links from Twitter's CDN safely.

Is it completely free?

Yes, Free X Video Saver is 100% free, has no hidden costs, and does not display any advertisements.

Does it support downloading GIFs?

Yes. Twitter converts GIFs into loopable MP4 files. The extension fully supports downloading these MP4 files directly to your device.

Do you store any history of downloaded videos?

No. The extension runs entirely client-side. We do not run any servers, and we have no access to your browsing history or downloaded files.
