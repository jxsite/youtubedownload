Frequently Asked Questions

How does the YouTube downloader extract resolutions?

The Chrome extension runs in the page context, safely reading player stream configurations (like adaptive streams for 1080p, 720p, and separate audio tracks) directly from the active YouTube HTML5 player.

What format do the transcripts come in?

You can download transcripts as clean paragraph Text (.txt), standard SubRip Subtitles (.srt), or WebVTT (.vtt) files, in any language currently available or auto-generated by the YouTube creator.

Is it completely free?

Yes, the extension and webpage are 100% free with no ads, no registration, and no tracking script.

Does it support downloading age-restricted videos?

Because the extension operates under your active browser session context, if you can watch the video in your current tab, the extension can resolve and download its resources.
